About the Conference

Welcome to the Data Science and Insurance Risk Analytics Conference 2025, hosted by the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science at KNUST, Kumasi, Ghana. Join us at the College of Science for an engaging event featuring keynote presentations, oral & poster presentations and networking opportunities.

Explore the latest predictive analytics methodologies transforming risk assessment

Conference Theme

"Transforming risk and resilience: predictive analytics in the future of actuarial science and statistics"

We invite experts and leaders in Statistics and Actuarial Science from across the world to present the newest and most impactful developments in the field. Join us as we turn risk into opportunities!
